Play. Prod. Hack. Discover.

We are StoneTurners. We like to prod the world, hack it apart then mash it back together. We like to play and get messy, then share our experiences, observations and creations.

Above all, we are curious and we want to discover things.

See the world in a whole new way with our USB Microscope

We’ve added a little twist the old ‘turn a webcam in to a microscope’ project. With our build instructions, some simple tools and little bit of time you'll be gaining a new perspective on the world.

To make things even easier we’ve also put together a kit you can buy from our store.

Our aim

We believe curiosity is a force for good and should be encouraged as an essential part of a life well lived.

We are born curious and ready to explore. It’s how we find things out, it’s how we learn. What’s more – it’s fun!

It’s our aim to help all the StoneTurners of this world to exercise their natural curiosity and encourage the desire to play, prod, hack and ultimately to discover.

What we do

We provide informative and inspirational content and instructions for how to make and access things that aid discovery.

We are inclusive, accessible and open source and aim to engender this approach in others.
